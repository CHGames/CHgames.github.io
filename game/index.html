Welcome to ninja madness!

An Awesome game created by Jett Green

There are 5 different charters

  • Ninja dude(that's you!)
  • Sensei(he's the guy telling you about your journey and all of that)
  • Samurai boss(huge fight at the end of the level)
  • evil apprentice(mini fight before the boss)
  • the minions(you find them and can be a bother)

    • There are five different levels...

    Each one does not get easier

    What to do/How to play...
    • There will be multiple choice questions and just type the answer that you think is correct, then let your destiny take over(make sure you add caps if there are any).
    • If you die you will have to restart the level
    • Remember to read this before playing
